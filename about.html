About Us

Festivity Hub is an event planning company dedicated to organizing and managing events for both individuals and organizations.

Our mission is to provide whatever experience our customers envisions and add much more to it.

We host around the University of the West Indies CaveHill campus and offer from parties to corporate seminars and even weddings and cultural events.

The team is passionate to attend to every detail and bring a unique approach that is appropiate to our client's needs
